[The human ark Hyperion arrives in the Andromeda galaxy.

634 YEARS LATER

DATE: 0 HOUR 0 DAY

 

[Multiple lights appear on different parts of the ship, indicating that some systems are now online.

and

[The technicians help Ryder get on his feet.]
