Mistakes[]

  • Despite the fact that there are only two adolescents present in the hologram, one male and one female, the names in the subtitles switch between two different titles for each of them.
    • Additionally, the girl is addressed by the multiservitor by name, Lena, but the subtitles still address her as Adolescent Female #1/2.
  • The transcript of this datapoint claims that a zapping sound takes place as Lena and the other adolescent attack the multiservitor and the Sentinels retaliate, when in reality, no such sound can be heard.
