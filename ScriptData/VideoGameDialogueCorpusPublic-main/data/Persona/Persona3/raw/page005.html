Ms. Toriumi: Oh...hm, I don't really like this author. Okay, nevermind. I've got a great poem we can cover instead. Close your textbooks, everyone, and listen..."Beneath the seas, high above in the skies..."



I thought about dozing off in class again, but I didn't. This was actually a really good idea, because...



...Ms. Toriumi is damn serious about her poetry.

Ms. Toriumi: You should be ashamed of yourself, taking a nap while I pour my heart into reading this poem! For that, you get another essay along with your regular assignment tonight! I'll expect a slice of cake when you turn it in, too!

Dodged a bullet there...well, I guess it's pretty ironic for me to say that, huh?



I didn't have anything to do after school, so I was free to do as I pleased, but there really wasn't that much available yet. Being the new kid sucks.

He had a decent selection, I guess...but I have to admit that I haven't had any weapons training, so I didn't know what exactly to get. What I think I'm gonna do is ask all of you guys on the Internet what weapon I should use every so often. That way I can mix it up a little and learn something about each...variety's the spice of life, huh?

Oh, when I asked Kurosawa why he didn't carry guns, he pointed at the Evoker at my waist and asked if I really wanted to risk pulling out the wrong gun in battle.

I didn't. Swords and bows are fine by me.



We left after that, having not bought anything. Junpei was in a shitty mood, so he took off, and I took the chance to look around the mall and learn about the stores.

Chagall Cafe is pretty busy most of the time, but they serve some sort of pheremone-laced coffee, which sounds pretty neat. (This raises Minato's Charm if we have him stay there in the afternoons.)

In the back of the mall, I found an entrance to the Velvet Room. I figured that, like the one in Tartarus, nobody but me could see this one either.



This is the Mandragora, a karaoke club. I'm actually a pretty big fan of karaoke...most people wouldn't guess that about me. (This raises Minato's Courage if we have him stay there in the afternoons.)
