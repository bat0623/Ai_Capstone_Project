Boss Battle: Contrarian King
Okay so this guy is much harder than you'd expect him to be. He's only got two real moves, Hysterical Slap and Rampage.
Hysterical Slap does a decent amount of damage to one target, and Rampage does massive damage to everyone. You are very likely to be
killed by Rampage if you're not around level 20 or so. He pulls a damn dirty trick at the start by casting Red Wall, which makes you
think he'll be weak to Fire. He actually Drains Fire, though. Having resistance to Physical helps, as does having a decent amount of
group healing and just in general being high enough level not to get gibbed by Rampage. The reward is a Courage boost and an awesome
weapon for Yukiko that gives her +50 SP so it's very worth doing this fight once you're high enough.

That thing would have been a real bastard if we'd not just come from some serious training, I'm glad we didn't tackle it earlier. Still, we were exhausted and had to head back.
