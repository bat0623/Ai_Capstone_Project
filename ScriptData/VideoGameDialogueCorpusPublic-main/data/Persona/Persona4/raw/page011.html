Boss Battle: Shadow Yukiko
And now the game has decided to stop playing the fuck around. Unlike the first two bosses, Shadow Yukiko has no weaknesses. She's totally immune to status effects, though she can be Downed by a crit. Her first turn is largely a wash, though you do want to be careful to have Chie guard so as not to let her get a One More right off the bat. When her second turn comes around, though…









…She summons a Charming Prince. The Charming Prince is a particularly annoying little bastard. It will buff itself and Shadow Yukiko with Sukukaja, it has Diarama, it can do some pretty nasty physical damage with its Cleave attack, and it can inflict Fear. That last one is actually by far the most dangerous, because if someone is Feared when Shadow Yukiko's turn comes around she can use an attack on them called Shivering Rondo. Shivering Rondo pretty much kills whoever it hits instantly. The Prince has one key flaw, though, he's weak against Ice. He also gets Dizzied by any hit on him while he's Down. We can keep him locked down for half of his turns, then, by simply hitting him with a Bufu skill and then whacking him during the One More. Personally, I like killing the Charming Prince before even really starting on Shadow Yukiko. One thing to be careful about is that if Shadow Yukiko gets a One More while he's up, she'll sometimes cast White Wall on the Prince to temporarily negate his weakness. If you do kill the Prince…







Why… Why won't he come…?
No one's coming! Now's your chance to attack!

Things are pretty easy for the first half of Yukiko's health bar. She'll just stick to her Agi, Double Fangs, and sometimes normal Attacks. These are merely damaging. It's when she gets to the second half of her health bar that things start to get entertaining. You'll know when you get there for two reasons.



That's not how it's supposed to work… You have to escort me!



She'll always use Burn To Ashes right after she ticks over into this phase, and she'll start using it every few turns or so from now on. There's no predicting it, she'll just do it whenever. If she gets One More in this phase, she'll use Matarunda on the party to reduce your offense. Things get even worse once her health gets critical.



You're no Princes… Die, you worthless commoners!

In the last bits of her health, she'll use Burn to Ashes even more often, and replace her Double Fang with Assault Dive. So how the hell do we deal with this? One common strategy uses a skill off of the Persona Slime, Red Wall. This can be used to negate Chie's Fire weakness. If this strategy has a weakness, it's the limited duration and relative expense of Red Wall. You'll really want to try and fuse it onto something other than Slime because Slime is himself weak to Fire. The strategy I use this time is to wear the Persona Senri, which is totally immune to Fire and can heal. This strategy's biggest weakness is that Senri has one of the lowest Endurance stats in the game, which makes you very vulnerable to Shadow Yukiko's physicals. Another strategy I like but didn't use this time is to get a Persona with Media (Valkyrie is a good source and indeed is a viable option). The group healing can really prove to be the difference between victory and defeat. If you're still having problems, you an always try to level up some more. The game expects you to be around level 15, if that's any help. Don't get discouraged, if you die a few times to this getting the hang of a real fight.

Shadow Yukiko was powerful well beyond anything we'd ever seen. I'm not sure how we defeated it.
