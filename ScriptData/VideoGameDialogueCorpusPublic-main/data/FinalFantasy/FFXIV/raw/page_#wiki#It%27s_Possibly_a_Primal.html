Return to the Waking Sands and inquire with the ever-wary administrator how you might be of aid.

  • Your arrival the Adders' Nest is welcomed by High Serpent Commander Heuloix. With time of the essence, he dispenses with pleasantries and immediately proceeds to explain the extenuating circumstances which compelled him to summon you. And so you steel yourself for grim news of what may lie ahead...
