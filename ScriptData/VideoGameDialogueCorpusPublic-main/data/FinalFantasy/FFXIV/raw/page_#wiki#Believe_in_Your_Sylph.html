  • Komuxio has asked you to observe his tempered kindred in the sylph homeland, hoping that your testimony might serve to reassure the Gridanians that they have nothing to fear from his people. Take yourself to the location he has marked on your map, and /lookout for tempered sylphs.

This quest can actually be accepted before finishing A Simple Gift

  • Talk to Komuxio and accept the quest.
  • Head east across the bridge and stand on the bright light on the ground and use the /lookout emote (without targeting anything).
  • Travel back to the Adders' Nest and hand the Letter from the Elder to Vorsaile Heuloix to complete the quest.
