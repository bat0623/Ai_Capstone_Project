  • Leofric has a request for an adventurer looking to do some good.
  • Leofric has asked you to deal with a band of outlaws who have taken up residence in the Quiveron Manse. The leader is a self-proclaimed baron, and you must dispose of him to send a message to his lackeys that banditry will not be tolerated.
